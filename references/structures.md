# Viral Carousel Structures

## The 5 Universal Viral Structures

### Structure 1 — Myth → Truth Framework
**Best for:** Niches with misinformation. High polarity.
**Goal:** Shares + reach

Slide flow:
1. Hook: "You've been lied to about _."
2. The myth everyone believes
3. What actually happens
4. Why this myth exists
5. The truth
6. Evidence or logic
7. What changes when you know this
8. Transformation
9. CTA
10. Final hook loop

**Why it works:** Polarity + clarity + insight

---

### Structure 2 — Here's Why You're Stuck Framework
**Best for:** Psychology, fitness, productivity, education
**Goal:** Comments + saves + followers

Slide flow:
1. Hook: "This is why you're not improving."
2. Symptom description
3. Misdiagnosis problem
4. Root cause
5. New insight
6. Example
7. Application
8. Transformation
9. CTA
10. Final loop

**Why it works:** People feel understood → they read the entire carousel

---

### Structure 3 — Micro Lessons Framework
**Best for:** Simple, repeatable content. Any niche.
**Goal:** Saves + shares

Slide flow:
1. Hook: "3 rules that change everything."
2. Rule #1
3. Rule #2
4. Rule #3
5. Short examples
6. Reinforcement
7. CTA
8–10. Final loop (optional)

**Why it works:** Short, snackable, highly shareable

---

### Structure 4 — Before → After Framework
**Best for:** Transformation-based niches
**Goal:** Shares + followers + emotional connection

Slide flow:
1. Hook: "If you're here, read this."
2. BEFORE: mindset/action/circumstance
3. PROBLEM: why it fails
4. SHIFT: the new approach
5. AFTER: expected change
6. Example
7. Reinforcement
8. CTA
9–10. Final loop

**Why it works:** Humans crave transformation

---

### Structure 5 — Warning Sign Framework
**Best for:** Any niche. Highest-performing format.
**Goal:** Reach + virality

Slide flow:
1. Hook: "If you're doing this — stop."
2. Sign #1
3. Why it's harmful
4. Sign #2
5. Why it's harmful
6. Sign #3
7. What to do instead
8. Transformation
9. CTA
10. Final loop

**Why it works:** Curiosity + fear of missing out

---

### Structure 6 — Before → After → Method (Educational)
**Best for:** Educational and transformational content
**Goal:** Saves + authority

Slide 1: Pain
Slide 2: Cause
Slide 3: Revelation
Slides 4–7: Method
Slide 8: Mini-Result
Slide 9: CTA

---

### Structure 7 — The List You Save
**Best for:** Practical, resource-type content
**Goal:** Saves

Use odd numbers: 3, 5, 7, 10 items
Hook example: "10 hooks you can use this week."

---

## How to Choose

| If goal is... | Use structure... |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Shares + reach | Myth → Truth or Warning Sign |
| Saves | Micro Lessons or The List |
| Followers | Here's Why You're Stuck |
| Comments | Warning Sign or Myth → Truth |
| Authority | Before → After → Method |
| Emotional trust | Before → After |
