# Hook Vault — 110 Fill-in-the-Blank Hooks

## The 7 Universal Hook Formulas

1. **Problem → Consequence** — "If you [do X]… this is why."
2. **Limiting Belief Hook** — "[X] isn't your problem. This is."
3. **Everyone Does This Wrong** — "[Common action] doesn't make you [result]."
4. **Nobody Talks About This** — "Your [X] isn't the issue. Your [Y] is."
5. **Routine That Creates the Problem** — "Your [positive habit] is making you [negative result]."
6. **Helpful Habit That Backfires** — "[Popular advice] is killing your [desired outcome]."
7. **One Small Change = Big Result** — "The micro-shift that [big result] instantly."

---

## Insight-Based Hook Formula (Write Original Hooks)
1. Identify an uncomfortable truth in the niche
2. Turn it into a sharp, direct sentence
3. Remove all unnecessary words
4. Add a consequence, twist, or new perspective

Examples:
- "You're not overwhelmed. You're unstructured."
- "Your diet isn't the issue. Your consistency is."
- "You don't need more time. You need more focus."

---

## 4 P's Hook Test (must hit 3 of 4)
- **Precision** — One clear idea
- **Problem** — Visible or implied
- **Polarity** — Fresh angle / unexpected truth
- **Perspective Shift** — Changes how reader sees something
