# Plug-and-Play Carousel Templates

## How to Use
1. Pick the template matching your idea
2. Fill in your topic/niche
3. Keep each slide to ONE idea
4. Add micro-curiosity between slides
5. End with clarity slide + soft CTA

---

## Template 1 — The "Shift" Carousel
*Ideal for big ideas, mindset changes, education*

- Slide 1: The belief your audience currently holds
- Slide 2: Why this belief fails them
- Slide 3: Introduce the real truth
- Slides 4–7: Break the truth into micro-insights
- Slide 8: Clear, concise summary
- Slide 9: "Save this to remember it."
- Slide 10: Soft CTA (optional)

---

## Template 2 — Before / After Insight
*Extremely shareable*

- Slide 1: "I used to think X… until I learned Y."
- Slide 2: Quick contrast of "before"
- Slides 3–6: The discoveries that changed everything
- Slide 7: What applying Y looks like now
- Slide 8: Mini example
- Slide 9: "Share this with someone who needs it."

---

## Template 3 — 3 Mistakes You Don't Realize You're Making

- Slide 1: "This is why you're stuck."
- Slides 2–4: Mistake 1, 2 and 3
- Slides 5–7: Fix 1, 2 and 3
- Slide 8: Micro-summary
- Slide 9: Soft CTA or "save for later"

---

## Template 4 — System Breakdown
*Perfect for coaches, educators, creators*

- Slide 1: "Here's the system you're missing."
- Slides 2–4: Step 1, Step 2, Step 3
- Slides 5–7: Example of the system in action
- Slide 8: Common mistakes when applying it
- Slide 9: Final takeaway

---

## Template 5 — Myth → Truth → Application

- Slide 1: A myth your audience believes
- Slide 2: Why this myth is popular
- Slide 3: The truth
- Slides 4–7: How to apply the truth
- Slide 8: Before/after scenario
- Slide 9: Soft CTA

---

## Template 6 — One Concept, Many Angles
*High save rate*

- Slide 1: One big idea
- Slides 2–5: The idea from 4 different angles
- Slides 6–8: Practical examples
- Slide 9: One-sentence summary

---

## Template 7 — Swipe Until It Clicks
*Designed for maximum retention*

- Slide 1: A bold statement
- Slides 2–8: Tiny insights that build clarity progressively
- Slide 9: Final crystallizing insight
- Slide 10: "Share if it clicked."

---

## Template 8 — Emotional Story Carousel

- Slide 1: A relatable moment
- Slides 2–4: What happened
- Slides 5–7: The shift or lesson
- Slide 8: The new perspective
- Slide 9: "Send this to someone who needs it."

---

## Template 9 — 3-Step Framework

- Slide 1: "This 3-step process changed everything."
- Slides 2–4: Step 1, Step 2, Step 3
- Slides 5–7: Specific examples for each
- Slide 8: Mistakes to avoid
- Slide 9: Summary

---

## Template 10 — Common Questions Answered

- Slide 1: "You keep asking me this…"
- Slides 2–8: Answer 5–7 common questions (one per slide)
- Slide 9: "Save this for later."

---

## Soft CTA Options (use on slide 9)
- "Save this before you forget it."
- "Share this with someone who needs it."
- "Comment [WORD] and I'll send you the full breakdown."
- "Follow for more on [topic]."
- "Send this to someone who's been struggling with [X]."
- "Tag someone who needs to hear this."

## Loop Close Options (use on slide 10)
Match back to your slide 1 hook:
- Slide 1: "You've been doing this wrong." → Slide 10: "Now you know why — and how to fix it."
- Slide 1: "This is why you're stuck." → Slide 10: "Now you have the way out."
- Slide 1: "Stop doing X." → Slide 10: "Start doing this instead."
